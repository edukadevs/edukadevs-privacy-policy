Edukadevs Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 16 October 2019, 02:20 AM (UTC+7)

Privacy

Edukadevs itself does not collect or store any user data. However, its current sole application, Edukade: Edukasi Adik-Adik, is made using MIT App Inventor, which may (but likely won't) ask for user data such as microphone access. Here, Edukadevs states that all such requests shall be denied, as Edukadevs does not use any user data.

Child Safety

As of right now, Edukadevs only has one application, namely Edukade: Edukasi Adik-Adik, in which children can learn children's learning material, such as letters, numbers and basic vocabulary. We state that as of right now, all of Edukadevs' services do not contain any explicit, graphical, or non-family-friendly content.

Implications Related to the Privacy Policy

By using any of Edukadevs' services, you are expected to have read and understood this privacy policy.
